The Book of Us : Entropy is the third Korean studio album of DAY6. It was released on October 22, 2019 with "Sweet Chaos" being the promotional title track and in two physical CD only editions: Sweet ver. and Chaos ver.
The Book of Us : The Demon is the sixth Korean studio album of DAY6. It was released on May 11, 2020 with "Zombie" being the promotional title track and in two physical CD only editions: MIDDAY Ver. and MIDNIGHT Ver.
The Book of Us : Negentropy - Chaos swallowed up in love (or simply The Book of Us : Negentropy) is the seventh Korean mini-album of DAY6. It was released on April 19, 2021 with "You make Me" serving as the promotional title track. The physical album was released in two types: One& version and Only version.
